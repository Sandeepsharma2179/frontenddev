Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favourite foods. Here is a reciepe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
